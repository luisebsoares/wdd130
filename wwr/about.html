Up Down Rafting

At Up Down Rafting, we are passionate about delivering the ultimate whitewater adventure. Whether you're a first-time rafter or a seasoned thrill-seeker, our expert guides ensure an exhilarating and safe experience. Navigate breathtaking rapids, soak in stunning natural landscapes, and create unforgettable memories on the river. Join us and feel the rush of adventure like never before!

Our History

Up Down Rafting was born out of a deep love for adventure and the great outdoors. More than 20 years ago, a group of passionate rafters decided to turn their thrilling weekend hobby into a full-fledged experience for others. Starting with just a single raft and a dream, they guided their first customers through gentle waters, building confidence and trust with every trip.

As word spread, so did our ambition. We sought out more exciting rapids, expanded our team with expert guides, and invested in top-tier equipment. Today, Up Down Rafting is a premier destination for thrill-seekers, families, and nature lovers alike. Whether you're looking for an adrenaline rush or a scenic river escape, we make every ride an unforgettable adventure.
